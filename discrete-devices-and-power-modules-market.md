<p><p>The discrete devices and power modules market encompasses various semiconductor components essential for power management. IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors) are favored for high voltage applications, while MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) excel in low-voltage, high-frequency environments. Diodes serve as critical components for rectification and protection. Intelligent Power Modules integrate multiple functions, improving efficiency and performance. Si/SiC hybrid modules combine silicon and silicon carbide technologies, offering superior thermal performance and efficiency, especially in demanding industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
