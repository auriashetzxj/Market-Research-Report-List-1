<p><p>Pharmaceutical packaging adhesives are critical for maintaining product integrity and safety. Solvent adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonds but may raise health concerns. Solvent-free adhesives eliminate these solvents, offering an environmentally friendly option with high performance. Waterborne adhesives use water as a solvent, leading to lower emissions and reduced toxicity, making them suitable for sensitive applications. Each type serves distinct needs in pharmaceutical packaging, balancing adhesion strength, safety, and environmental impact.</p></p>
