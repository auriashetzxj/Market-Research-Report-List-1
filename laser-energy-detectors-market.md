<p><strong>The Laser Energy Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric</li><li>Photodiode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser energy detectors market primarily includes two types: pyroelectric and photodiode detectors. Pyroelectric detectors measure energy by sensing temperature changes caused by laser absorption, making them suitable for pulsed laser applications. They offer high sensitivity and a wide spectral range. On the other hand, photodiode detectors convert light energy directly into an electrical signal, providing real-time measurement capabilities. Photodiodes are ideal for continuous wave lasers and offer fast response times. Both types are essential for various industrial, medical, and research applications.</p></p>
