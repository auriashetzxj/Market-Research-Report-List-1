<p><p>The portable color ultrasound equipment market is categorized into 2D, 3D, 4D, and Doppler types. 2D ultrasound provides cross-sectional images for basic diagnostics. 3D ultrasound offers volumetric imaging, enhancing visualization of anatomy, while 4D ultrasound adds real-time motion, useful in obstetrics. Doppler ultrasound focuses on blood flow analysis, crucial for assessing cardiovascular health. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, contributing to the expanding adoption of portable ultrasound technologies in diverse healthcare settings. </p></p>
