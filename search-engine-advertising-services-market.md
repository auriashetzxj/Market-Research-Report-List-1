<p><p>Search Engine Advertising Services encompass various pricing models that cater to different marketing strategies. CPT (Cost Per Time) charges advertisers based on the duration their ads are displayed. CPA (Cost-Per-Acquisition) focuses on payment only when a specific action, like a sale, occurs. CPC (Cost-Per-Click) charges advertisers for each click their ad receives. CPM (Cost-Per-Thousand-Impressions) bills based on how many times an ad is shown, while other models may include variations or combinations of these approaches to optimize advertising effectiveness.</p></p>
