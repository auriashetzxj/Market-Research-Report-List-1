<p><ul><li>Implantable Ocular Drug Delivery Systems</li><li>Particulate Drug Delivery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ocular drug delivery system market includes implantable ocular drug delivery systems and particulate drug delivery systems. Implantable systems involve devices surgically placed within the eye to release medication over an extended period, enhancing treatment efficacy for chronic conditions like glaucoma. Particulate systems utilize micrometer or nanoparticle formulations for targeted delivery, improving drug solubility and bioavailability while minimizing systemic side effects. These innovative approaches aim to enhance therapeutic outcomes and patient compliance in managing ocular diseases.</p></p>
