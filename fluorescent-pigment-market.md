<p><p>The fluorescent pigment market is divided into several types based on their formulation. Thermoset types are heat-cured, offering durability and resistance to harsh conditions, making them suitable for coatings and plastics. Thermoplastic types remain pliable and can be reshaped upon heating, ideal for applications like ink and flexible materials. Other categories include aqueous dispersions, which are water-based and environmentally friendly, used in various applications such as paints and textiles, offering versatility and ease of use.</p></p>
