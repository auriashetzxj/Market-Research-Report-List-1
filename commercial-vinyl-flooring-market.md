<p><strong>The Commercial Vinyl Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring</li><li>Homogeneous Sheet Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vinyl flooring market features two main types: heterogeneous sheet flooring and homogeneous sheet flooring. Heterogeneous sheet flooring consists of multiple layers, combining a vinyl wear layer, print design, and backing, offering diverse aesthetics and enhanced durability against wear and tear. In contrast, homogeneous sheet flooring is made from a single-layer composition, ensuring uniformity in texture and color throughout, making it highly durable and easy to maintain. Both types are favored for their resilience and versatility in commercial spaces.</p></p>
