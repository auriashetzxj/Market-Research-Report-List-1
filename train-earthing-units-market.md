<p><strong>The Train Earthing Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axle Earth Return Units</li><li>Earth Return Brushes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Train Earthing Units market comprises two primary types: Axle Earth Return Units and Earth Return Brushes. Axle Earth Return Units facilitate the safe dissipation of electrical currents through the wheel axles of rail vehicles, ensuring efficient earth return paths. In contrast, Earth Return Brushes provide additional connectivity between the train’s structure and the track, enhancing electrical grounding. Both systems are crucial for maintaining safety and performance in railway operations, minimizing risks associated with electrical faults.</p></p>
