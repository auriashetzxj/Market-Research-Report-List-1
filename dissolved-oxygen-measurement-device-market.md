<p><p>Dissolved oxygen measurement devices are critical in environmental monitoring and aquatic systems. The market can be segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Polarographic**: These utilize a cathode and anode to measure oxygen levels, requiring calibration and maintenance.</p><p>2. **Galvanic**: Similar to polarographic devices but generate their electrical current without external power, offering simplicity in use.</p><p>3. **Optical (Luminescent)**: These employ luminescent materials that fluoresce in response to oxygen, providing accurate readings without the need for frequent maintenance.</p></p>
