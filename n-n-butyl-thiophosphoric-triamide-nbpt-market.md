<p><p>N-(n-butyl) Thiophosphoric Triamide (NBPT) is a specialized nitrogen stabilizer primarily used in agriculture to enhance the efficiency of urea-based fertilizers. As environmental concerns regarding nitrogen management and fertilizer efficiency intensify, the demand for NBPT is expected to rise.</p><p>The market growth analysis for NBPT reflects a positive trajectory driven by increasing agricultural productivity needs and a shift towards sustainable farming practices.
