<p><p>The threaded couplings market consists of various types, primarily threaded half couplings and threaded full couplings. Threaded half couplings are designed for connecting pipes with a male thread, allowing for a secure and compact joint in piping systems. In contrast, threaded full couplings join two pipes with female threads, providing a robust connection that accommodates various diameters. Both types are essential in plumbing, industrial applications, and infrastructure, ensuring effective fluid transfer and system integrity across various sectors.</p></p>
