Flush toilets operate using gravity to deliver water directly into the bowl, providing a straightforward and efficient waste removal system. Siphon toilets, on the other hand, utilize a siphoning action to enhance flushing performance, often resulting in a quieter operation and reduced water usage. Both types offer modern aesthetics and space-saving advantages, making them popular choices in contemporary bathroom designs.</p></p>
