<p><ul><li>Internal Fixator</li><li>External Fixator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printed maxillofacial implant market comprises two main types: internal fixators and external fixators. Internal fixators are devices implanted within the body to stabilize facial structures following trauma or surgery, providing support while promoting natural healing. Conversely, external fixators are mounted outside the body and are typically used to assist in the alignment and stabilization of facial bones during the recovery process. Both types leverage 3D printing technology for customized designs that enhance patient-specific fit and overall outcomes.</p></p>
