<p><p>The passenger vehicle diesel engine intake valve market comprises three main types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single material, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Bimetallic valves combine different metals for enhanced thermal resistance and durability. Hollow valves incorporate a hollow structure, reducing weight and enhancing performance while maintaining strength. Each type serves unique applications, catering to the diverse demands of modern diesel engines, improving efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
