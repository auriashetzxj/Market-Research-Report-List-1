<p><strong>The UV Cured Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Curing</li><li>LED Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV cured printing inks utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly cure or harden inks during the printing process. The market primarily comprises two types: Arc Curing and LED Curing. Arc Curing employs traditional mercury vapor lamps, producing intense UV light suitable for high-speed applications. Meanwhile, LED Curing utilizes energy-efficient, longer-lasting LED lights, offering lower heat emission and a wider range of substrate compatibility. Both technologies enhance print durability and quality, meeting the demands of various commercial and industrial printing applications.</p></p>
