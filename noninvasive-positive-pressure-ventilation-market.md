<p><p>The Noninvasive Positive-pressure Ventilation (NPPV) market includes several types of devices. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) delivers a steady airflow to keep airways open during sleep. Automatic Positive Airway Pressure (autoPAP) adapts pressure levels in real time based on breathing patterns. Dual Horizontal Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) provides two different pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, facilitating easier breathing. Additionally, other devices encompass specialized ventilators for specific respiratory needs, enhancing patient care and comfort in noninvasive ventilation.</p></p>
