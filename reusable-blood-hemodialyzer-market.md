<p><ul><li>Single Pump</li><li>Double Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reusable blood hemodialyzer market is segmented into single pump and double pump systems. Single pump hemodialyzers utilize one pump to circulate blood and dialysate, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, double pump systems employ two pumps, one for blood and another for dialysate, allowing for more precise control and improved efficiency in the dialysis process. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing the choice based on patient requirements and treatment settings.</p></p>
