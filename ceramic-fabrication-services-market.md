<p><p>Ceramic fabrication services encompass various processes to shape and manufacture ceramic materials. Casting involves pouring liquid ceramic into molds to achieve desired shapes. Grinding refines the surface of ceramic parts for precision and smoothness. Injection molding combines ceramic powder with a binder to create complex shapes through a mold process. Machining entails cutting and shaping hard ceramic materials to precise specifications. Other methods may include additive manufacturing and slip casting, expanding the versatility of ceramic applications across industries.</p></p>
