<p><ul><li>Static Var Compensator (SVC)</li><li>Static Var Generator (SVG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reactive Power Compensation Device market includes Static Var Compensators (SVC) and Static Var Generators (SVG). SVCs employ capacitors and reactors to maintain voltage stability in power systems by dynamically adjusting reactive power. Conversely, SVGs utilize power electronics to generate and absorb reactive power, providing faster and more precise control. Both devices enhance grid reliability, improve power quality, and support renewable integration, catering to the growing demand for efficient and resilient electrical networks across various industries.</p></p>
