<p><p>The Solar Control Window Films market consists of three primary types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films offer minimal tint, providing UV protection while maintaining visibility. Dyed films, infused with color, reduce glare and heat without reflective properties, making them ideal for privacy. Vacuum-coated films utilize reflective surfaces to significantly block solar energy, enhancing thermal comfort and energy efficiency. Each type serves distinct purposes in enhancing indoor environments and protecting interiors from harmful UV rays.</p></p>
