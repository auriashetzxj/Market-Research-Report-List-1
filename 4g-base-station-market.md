<p><p>The 4G base station market comprises two primary types: macrocells and small cells. Macrocells are large towers that provide extensive coverage over wide areas, suitable for urban and rural environments. They handle substantial traffic and support many users simultaneously. Small cells, on the other hand, are compact stations designed for dense urban areas, enhancing capacity and coverage in specific locations. These are ideal for boosting signals in buildings or crowded places, complementing macrocells to create a robust network.</p></p>
