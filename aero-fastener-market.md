<p><p>The Aero Fastener market is primarily divided into two categories: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, including bolts, screws, and nuts, facilitate secure connections and allow for adjustments and disassembly, making them essential in various aerospace applications. Non-threaded fasteners, such as rivets and pins, are used for permanent joining and offer enhanced stability under dynamic loads. Both types play critical roles in ensuring the safety, durability, and performance of aircraft structures and components in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
