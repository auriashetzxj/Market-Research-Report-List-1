<p><strong>The Thermally Conductive Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermally Conductive Insulating Plastic</li><li>Thermally And Electrically Conductive Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermally conductive plastics market consists of two main types: thermally conductive insulating plastics and thermally and electrically conductive plastics. Thermally conductive insulating plastics are designed to dissipate heat while preventing electrical conductivity, ideal for applications requiring heat management without electrical interference. In contrast, thermally and electrically conductive plastics facilitate both heat dissipation and electrical conductivity, making them suitable for devices where heat and electrical performance are critical, such as in electronics and automotive components.</p></p>
