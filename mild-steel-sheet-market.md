<p><p>The Mild Steel Sheet market is primarily divided into two types: Hot Roll Mild Steel Sheets and Cold Roll Mild Steel Sheets. Hot Roll Mild Steel Sheets are produced through high-temperature processes, offering greater thickness and strength, suitable for structural applications. In contrast, Cold Roll Mild Steel Sheets are processed at room temperature, resulting in smoother finishes and tighter tolerances, ideal for applications where aesthetics and precision are critical. Both types meet diverse industrial needs and are widely used in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
