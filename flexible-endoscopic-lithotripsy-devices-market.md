<p><p>The flexible endoscopic lithotripsy devices market is categorized into three main types: ultrasonic lithotripsy, ballistic lithotripsy, and others. Ultrasonic lithotripsy employs high-frequency sound waves to disintegrate stones, offering precise targeting with minimal tissue damage. Ballistic lithotripsy uses kinetic energy from projectiles to break stones and is known for its effectiveness in larger stones. The "others" category encompasses various emerging technologies and methods that enhance lithotripsy procedures, reflecting continuous innovation in the field to improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
