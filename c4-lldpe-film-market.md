<p><p>C4 LLDPE film market types primarily include blown film extrusion and cast film extrusion. Blown film extrusion involves creating films through the inflation of molten polymer, resulting in properties like strength and flexibility, ideal for packaging. Cast film extrusion, on the other hand, entails cooling the polymer on a flat surface, producing films with smoother surfaces and better clarity, suitable for applications like bags and packaging. Additionally, other methods may encompass niche technologies or specialty films that cater to specific market demands.</p></p>
