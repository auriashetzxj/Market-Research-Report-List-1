<p><p>Mobile Computerized Tomography (CT) markets are categorized into Full-body Scanning and Partial Scanning segments. Full-body scanning provides comprehensive imaging of the entire body, facilitating detailed diagnosis for conditions such as cancer, trauma, or internal bleeding. In contrast, Partial Scanning focuses on specific body parts, offering targeted imaging for conditions like fractures or localized tumors. Both segments serve distinct clinical needs, enhancing diagnostic capabilities while maintaining patient comfort and minimizing exposure to radiation.</p></p>
