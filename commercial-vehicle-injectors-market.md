<p><p>The commercial vehicle injector market includes two primary types: gasoline direct injectors and diesel direct injectors. Gasoline direct injectors deliver fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing engine efficiency and reducing emissions. They are favored in modern gasoline engines for improved performance. Diesel direct injectors, on the other hand, spray fuel directly into the combustion chamber of diesel engines, ensuring precise fuel delivery for optimal combustion and power output. Both types play a critical role in meeting stringent environmental regulations and performance standards.</p></p>
