<p><p>Digital radiography in chest radiography involves advanced imaging techniques that enhance the visualization of lung and heart conditions. The market is segmented into two primary types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Direct Radiography (DR). CR technology utilizes imaging plates to capture data, which is then processed digitally, offering flexibility and integration with existing systems. In contrast, DR technology directly converts X-ray energy into digital signals, providing faster imaging and improved image quality, making it ideal for high-volume healthcare settings.</p></p>
