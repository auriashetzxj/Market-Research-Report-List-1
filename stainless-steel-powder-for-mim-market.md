<p><p>The stainless steel powder market for metal injection molding (MIM) is categorized into three main grades: austenitic, martensitic, and ferritic. Austenitic grades, known for their excellent corrosion resistance and ductility, are widely used in various applications. Martensitic grades offer higher strength and hardness, making them suitable for tools and structural components. Ferritic grades, characterized by better stress corrosion resistance and magnetic properties, are often utilized in automotive and industrial applications. Each grade serves distinct functionalities in MIM processes.</p></p>
