<p><p>The fire-rated glass market includes various types designed to enhance safety in buildings. Laminated fire-resistant glass consists of multiple layers for improved thermal and impact resistance. Tempered fire-resistant glass is treated for increased strength and withstands high temperatures. Wired fire-resistant glass features embedded wires, providing structural stability and delaying heat transfer. Ceramic fire-resistant glass offers superior heat resistance with minimal thermal expansion, making it ideal for high-temperature environments. Each type serves specific applications in fire-rated constructions, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
